Dress Code
Servicemembers
Soldiers assigned to the 111th MI BDE will attend in uniform.
Soldiers attending as guests of 111th MI BDE personnel may wear formal civilian attire or dress uniforms.
Soldiers are authorized to wear any of the mess uniforms, the Army Service Uniform dress variation, or the Army Green Service Uniform dress variation.
"Party shirts" are authorized, and all Servicemembers are authorized to change into formal civilian attire (preferably matching the night's theme) after the colors have been retired.
Civilians
Black-tie appropriate formal wear, preferably matching the evening's theme - Old Hollywood Glamour.
